Griffin officially disbands League of Legends team



BY GLORIA BORGER, DECEMBER 5 2021

 
 
               Korean League of Legends team Griffin is disbanding entirely effective immediately, the organization announced today.
               Just two seasons ago, Griffin reached the quarterfinals at the 2019 World Championship. 
               A storybook team full of young prospects was able to carry the budding organization into the limelight.
               “With the dream-like team behind, the journey of Griffin LoL has come to an end,” the org said. 
               “We would like to express our endless thanks to the fans who have supported Team Griffin.” Via Twitter:
